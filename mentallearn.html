Mental Illness



Mental Illness is a big problem and it can happen to anyone. It is time that we erase the stigma that surrounds mental illness and raise awareness for Mental Illness instead.

The organizations below will help allow to contribute and fix this problem:

  • NAMI

    • NAMI, the National Alliance on Mental Illness, is an organization that strives to bring awareness to the seriousness of mental illness and mental health. It educates people and implements public policies for people with mental illnesses. To learn more, click on the image below!



  • MHA

    • The Mental Health in America organization was founded in 1909 and is one of the is the nation's leading community-based nonprofit dedicated to addressing the needs of those living with mental illness and to promoting the overall mental health of all Americans.
