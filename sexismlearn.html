Gender/Sexuality Equality



Sexism, which is defined as prejudice, stereotyping, or discrimination towards a certain sex and sexuality, has become a greater problem as more people are allowed to be themselves. Sexism is not just confined to prejudice against women, but can include prejudice against the LGBTQ + A community.esent since the begginning of time. Even if we have become more progressive, there is still much room to create equality and spread love.

The organizations below will help allow to contribute and fix this problem:

  • It Gets Better Project

    • The It Gets Better Project was created to encourage LGBTQ youths to follow their dreams. The It Gets Better Project's mission is to communicate to lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender youth around the world that it gets better, and to create and inspire the changes needed to make it better for them.To learn more, click on the image below!



  • Malala Fund

    • The Malala Fund was created by notable, Malala Yousefzai, to encourage girls and women to pursue their dreams and get an education. Its goal is to invest in local education leaders and programmes and advocate for more resources for education and safe schools for every child.Click the picture below to learn more!

  • GLSEN

    • GLSEN, which stands for the Gay, Lesbian & Straight Education Network,is an organization which strives to assure that each member of every school community is valued and respected regardless of sexual orientation or gender identity/expression. It aims to create safe educational environments for LGBT youth. Click the picture below to learn more!

  • Girls Who Code

    • Girls Who Code is a non-profit organization which tries to close the gender gap between men and women in technology. It tries to get more girls interested in computer science. People can get involved by starting a club or joining the Summer immersion Program.Click the picture below to learn more!
